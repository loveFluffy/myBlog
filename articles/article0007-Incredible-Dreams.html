Article#0007: Incredible Dreams

Then gradually I grew older, I got the same problem, but thankfully that is infrequent. Thinking back, all the incredible dreams she told then was one of the few sources of my bedtime stories.

Here I'll write my own incredible dreams. As time pass by, all dreams will finally fade if I don't make notes. So I decide to write them down or even paint if I have to.
